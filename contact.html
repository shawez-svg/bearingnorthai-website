Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can you start a project?

We can typically begin initial discovery and planning within 1-2 weeks of engagement. Full project timelines vary based on scope and complexity.

Do you work with companies outside Dubai?

Absolutely! We serve clients across all UAE emirates including Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, and beyond. We also support select clients in the broader GCC region.

What is your pricing model?

We offer flexible engagement models including fixed-price projects, time & materials, and retained advisory services. Pricing depends on project scope, complexity, and duration.

Do you provide ongoing support after implementation?

Yes! We offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages to ensure your AI systems continue performing optimally. This includes monitoring, updates, and continuous optimization.

Can you work with our existing technology stack?

We're technology-agnostic and experienced in integrating with diverse technology stacks. We'll assess your infrastructure and recommend the best integration approach.

How do you ensure data privacy and compliance?

We adhere to strict data protection protocols, UAE regulations, and international standards (GDPR, ISO 27001). All projects include comprehensive compliance and security assessments.
